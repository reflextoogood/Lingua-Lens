LinguaLens stores your API key and provider settings locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. We do not collect, transmit, or have access to your API key or any data. Subtitle text and clicked words are sent directly from your browser to the LLM provider you configure (e.g., Anthropic, OpenAI, OpenRouter) to generate explanations. LinguaLens does not operate any servers and has no access to your usage data.
